About the Portfolio

The Origin

Pines Finance started because I wanted to understand how markets actually move when the noise clears. Textbook theory only takes you so far. I built this platform as an open research register to force myself to dive into real market mechanics, test different analytical frameworks, and share my raw findings with anyone tracking the same trends. It’s my space to turn financial curiosity into structured, public projects.

The Workflow

My workflow is built around exploring unknown territory. I start by isolating a core financial concept from which I formulate a specific, targeted research question. To make sure I am constantly expanding my perspective, I deliberately choose a completely new sector or industry for each paper so I can master its unique operational drivers. Once the industry is locked in, I gather historical market data, map the numbers out in Excel to isolate anomalies or trends, and translate those quantitative results into APA-formatted research papers.

The Scale

This platform is built to scale. Moving forward, my target is to expand the range and depth of my data tracking arrays to backtest my results with higher accuracy. I am currently mapping out deeper analytical pipelines focused on corporate asset structures and risk premiums. Ultimately, I want this platform to grow into an educational hub that helps other student analysts cut through the fluff and look directly at the math, and inspire others to start building their own models instead of just reading about them.
